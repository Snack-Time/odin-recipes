Sloppy Joes

Description

This recipe will show you how to make Sloppy Joe sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds extra lean ground beef
  • 1/4 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup water
  • 3/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 dash Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine the ground beef and onion in a cold skillet, place the skillet onto a stove burner, and turn the heat to medium; cook and stir until the beef is crumbly and browned.
  2. Stir the garlic and bell pepper into the beef mixture; continue cooking and stirring until the vegetables are tender, 2 to 3 more minutes.
  3. Add 1 cup of water.
  4. Mix in ketchup, brown sugar, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour in 1 more cup of water. Bring to a simmer.
  6. Reduce heat to low and cook until the mixture becomes very thick, 30 to 45 minutes. Adjust salt and pepper as needed. Season with cayenne.
